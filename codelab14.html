Go Further

Congratulations for having completed the two exercises! Now for some 'go further' exercise after a long time:

  • There are a lot of operators in Python similar to != and ==. Try to find out as many "equality operators" as you can. They should be like: < or <=.
  • Play with the python by typing out new boolean operators, and before you hit the enter try to shout out what it is. Do not think about it, just the first thing that comes to mind. Write them down then hit enter, and keep track of how many you get write and wrong.
